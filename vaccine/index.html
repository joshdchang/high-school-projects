New York State Vaccine Appointment Finder

By Josh Chang

Instructions:

  1. Make sure you are using the Firefox browser.
  2. Download the add-on.
  3. Go to https://am-i-eligible.covid19vaccine.health.ny.gov/ and fill out the form to see if you are eligible.
  4. Click on "Schedule your vaccine appointment" for the distribution location closest to you, even if it says "No Appointments Available Currently".
    THIS MAY BE BELOW LOCATIONS THAT ARE FURTHER FROM YOU BUT HAVE APPOINTMENTS AVAILABLE.
  5. Complete any "I am not a robot" checks that may appear.
  6. The add-on will reload until it finds an appointment in the next week, at which point it will change the name of the tab to "Found!".
  7. If you are available for the appointment, book it, otherwise go back to step 4 and try again.
  8. If you encounter any errors, go back to step 4.
  9. Enjoy!
